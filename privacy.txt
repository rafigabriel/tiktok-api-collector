# Privacy Policy - TikTok Collector

Last updated: March 2025

TikTok Collector is a research tool designed to collect publicly available information from TikTok profiles and videos.

## 1. Data Collection
We collect only publicly available data from TikTok’s API, such as:
- Profile information (username, followers, etc.).
- Video metadata (views, likes, etc.).

## 2. Data Usage
The collected data is used solely for research and analytical purposes. No personally identifiable information is stored beyond what is publicly accessible.

## 3. Data Sharing
We do not share, sell, or distribute collected data to third parties.

## 4. Security
This tool does not store sensitive data or login credentials. All API requests follow TikTok’s official security guidelines.

## 5. Changes to Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of this software means you accept the updated policy.

For any concerns, contact the developer.
